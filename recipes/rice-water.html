Rice Water

A Nutritious Bowl of Rice Water

This is a popular African meal made from rice and hot water, hence its name.

You gain much energy as you consume it with bread and keep going about your daily duties.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Powdered chocolate

Steps

  1. Put water on fire to the bioiling point.
  2. Add rice to it and wait for 10 minutes.
  3. Then begin to stir until cooked.
  4. Serve into a bowl, add milk and powered chocolate.
  5. Enjoy!
